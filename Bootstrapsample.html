About

Wing It is a website solely dedicated to beauty - the products we use, love, adore, sometimes abhor, complete with the stories and people behind them.

We like to think that the subject of beauty is a fascinating, complex and never ending tale, and just like you we can't get enough of the latest launches, cult products or celebrity must-haves; the difference is we can't help but document our adventures along the way and hope you'll join us in discoveries both old and new.
